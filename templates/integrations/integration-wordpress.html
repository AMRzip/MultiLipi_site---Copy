Frequently Asked Questions

Is MultiLipi compatible with WordPress websites?
Yes! MultiLipi is fully compatible with WordPress. You can translate your WordPress website into 120+ languages within minutes — no coding required. Just install the plugin, choose your languages, and go live instantly.
How is MultiLipi different from other translation plugins?
MultiLipi offers a simpler setup, intuitive dashboard, and AI + human-powered translations, all while focusing on multilingual SEO. Unlike many competitors, we provide real-time editing, subdomain/subdirectory support, and seamless plugin compatibility.
How do I integrate MultiLipi with my WordPress website?
It’s easy! Install the MultiLipi WordPress translation plugin, select your preferred languages, and follow our quick start guide. Your multilingual site will be ready in less than 5 minutes.
Can I try MultiLipi for free on WordPress?
Absolutely. You can start with our free plan, which allows you to test MultiLipi on your WordPress site without any commitment. Upgrade anytime for more words, languages, and advanced features.
How does MultiLipi work on WordPress?
MultiLipi automatically detects and translates your website content using AI. You can then edit or enhance translations manually via the MultiLipi dashboard, or invite human translators. It's a complete multilingual WordPress solution.
Is MultiLipi good for WordPress multilingual SEO?
Yes! MultiLipi follows Google’s best practices for multilingual SEO — including hreflang tags, translated metadata, and support for language-specific subdomains or subdirectories. This helps your translated WordPress website rank globally.
