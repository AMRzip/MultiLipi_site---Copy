MultiLipi stands out with its easy onboarding, real-time translation preview, AI + human editing support, and built-in multilingual SEO features. You get full control and scalability—perfect for businesses of any size.

Integration is simple! Just copy-paste a lightweight script into your Wix site settings, select your languages, and you’re live. Follow our step-by-step guide to launch your multilingual Wix website in under 5 minutes.

Absolutely. MultiLipi follows Google’s multilingual SEO best practices, including automatic hreflang tags, translated metadata, and support for language-specific URLs—helping your translated Wix site rank in global search results.
