Frequently Asked Questions

Is MultiLipi compatible with Shopify websites?
Yes! MultiLipi is fully compatible with Shopify. You can make your store multilingual in just a few minutes — no coding needed. It integrates smoothly with your product pages, collections, and checkout for a seamless customer experience.
How is MultiLipi different from other translation apps?
MultiLipi offers a faster setup, more flexible editing options, and a dedicated focus on multilingual SEO. You get AI-powered translation, manual overrides, and a clean interface — making it ideal for growing Shopify businesses.
How do I set up MultiLipi on my Shopify store?
Setting up MultiLipi is simple. Just add our lightweight script to your Shopify theme or use our step-by-step Shopify integration guide. Select your languages, and your translated site goes live instantly.
Can I try MultiLipi for free on Shopify?
Absolutely. MultiLipi comes with a free plan so you can explore core features, translate your site, and test multilingual SEO. Upgrade anytime for more languages, translated words, and advanced tools.
How does MultiLipi work with Shopify?
MultiLipi automatically detects and translates all your Shopify content — from product descriptions to checkout messages. You can manage and refine translations in the dashboard, invite collaborators, and control how your store appears in every language.
Is MultiLipi optimized for Shopify multilingual SEO?
Yes. MultiLipi includes all essential SEO features for multilingual Shopify stores — like hreflang tags, translated metadata, and language-specific URLs. It follows Google’s best practices to help your store rank globally.
