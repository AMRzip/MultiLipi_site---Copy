Frequently Asked Questions

Can I translate my entire eCommerce store with MultiLipi?
Yes! MultiLipi automatically detects and translates all parts of your eCommerce store — including product pages, categories, checkout flow, popups, and 3rd-party app content. You can edit and manage all translations from one centralized dashboard.
How fast can I make my store multilingual with MultiLipi?
You can get your multilingual eCommerce website live in just a few minutes. Simply select the languages you want to support, and MultiLipi handles translation instantly using AI, with optional manual refinement.
Does MultiLipi support multilingual SEO for product pages?
Absolutely. MultiLipi follows all multilingual SEO best practices, including hreflang tags, language-specific URLs, and translated metadata — helping your product and category pages rank internationally.
Will my product descriptions and variations be translated too?
Yes, MultiLipi translates product titles, descriptions, attributes, and variants. It works across most eCommerce platforms and supports dynamic content from your CMS or plugin ecosystem.
How does MultiLipi handle updates to my store content?
Any new product, page, or content you add to your store is automatically detected and translated by MultiLipi. Your content remains up to date in every language without needing to manage multiple stores.
Can I manually edit translations for accuracy or tone?
Yes! While MultiLipi provides AI-powered translations, you can manually edit any content or invite team members and translators to review and customize the messaging for different markets.
Will using MultiLipi slow down my store?
No. MultiLipi is optimized for performance and delivers translations without affecting your store speed. It works efficiently with major eCommerce platforms and page builders.
