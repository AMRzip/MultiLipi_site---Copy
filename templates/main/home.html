Frequently Asked Questions

How many languages can I translate my website into?
With MultiLipi, you can translate your website into 120+ languages, including 22 Indian languages such as Hindi, Tamil, Gujarati, and Marathi. Whether you're building a multilingual site for regional or global users, we've got you covered.
What is a language subdomain or subdirectory?
For multilingual SEO, MultiLipi supports both subdirectories and subdomains.
● Example subdirectory: www.example.com/hi/ (for Hindi)
● Example subdomain: hi.example.com
These structures help search engines index your translated website correctly and improve visibility in local search results.
How is MultiLipi optimized for multilingual SEO?
MultiLipi automatically implements Google-recommended SEO practices like:
● Language-specific subdomains/subdirectories
● Hreflang tag generation
● Translation of page metadata
These features ensure your translated website ranks well and maintains SEO consistency across every language.
How accurate is MultiLipi's machine translation?
MultiLipi uses AI-powered neural machine translation for high-quality results. You also get full control through the dashboard to refine translations manually or with human editors, ensuring accurate website translation.
How can I edit the machine-translated content?
Use the MultiLipi Dashboard to edit any translation instantly. You can make changes yourself or invite team members and professional translators to review and update content for your multilingual site.
Where are my website translations stored?
All your translated website content is securely stored within the MultiLipi platform. The dashboard gives you a clear, organized view of every language version, making it easy to manage updates.
How do I know how many translated words I need?
The number of translated words depends on:
● Total word count on your current website
● Number of target languages
Use our Word Count Tool to calculate your needs, and we'll suggest the right MultiLipi plan based on your website translation goals.
Can I use MultiLipi on more than one website?
Yes! With the MultiLipi Pro Plan or higher, you can manage multiple websites independently. Each site can have its own translations and user access — ideal for agencies or large businesses.
