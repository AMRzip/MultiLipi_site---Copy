Privacy Policy

Last updated on Feb 15th 2025

1. Introduction

MULTILIPI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED ("we," "us," or "our") is deeply committed to safeguarding personal data and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws. This Privacy Policy details the categories of personal data we collect, the purposes for which such data is processed, the legal foundations underpinning such processing, and the security mechanisms in place to protect the confidentiality and integrity of user information.

By accessing our website and utilizing our services, you acknowledge and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy. We strongly encourage users to read this policy carefully to understand how we handle their personal information.

2. Data Controller Information

MULTILIPI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED serves as the data controller responsible for determining the purposes and means of processing personal data. Should you have any inquiries regarding data protection or privacy rights, please contact:

  • Email: support@multilipi.com
  • Address: 75/17, Tagore Lane, Mansarovar, Jaipur 302020

3. Categories of Personal Data Collected

We collect, store, and process various types of personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Identity Data: Full name, job title, company affiliation
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Demographic Data: Postal code, preferences, interests, age range
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, login timestamps
  • Usage Data: Website navigation behavior, session duration, interaction with services
  • Marketing Preferences: Subscription preferences, communication history

We do not collect or process sensitive personal data unless explicitly required by applicable law.

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Our processing of personal data is conducted in accordance with GDPR’s legal bases, including but not limited to:

  • Consent: Explicit user consent obtained for specific processing activities.
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to fulfill contractual obligations.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our business interests, provided these do not override data subject rights.
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements.

5. Purpose of Data Processing

We use personal data for the following lawful purposes:

  • To provide, manage, and improve our services
  • To maintain internal records and ensure operational efficiency
  • To send marketing communications and promotional offers (subject to consent)
  • To conduct market research and analyze user engagement
  • To personalize website content and optimize user experience
  • To fulfill our legal and regulatory obligations

6. Data Retention Policy

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Upon expiration of the retention period, data is securely deleted, anonymized, or archived in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

7. Data Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures to ensure the protection of personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption protocols, access control systems, and periodic security audits.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website utilizes cookies and tracking technologies to enhance user experience and improve website performance.

  • Types of Cookies: Essential, performance, functional, analytics, and marketing cookies.
  • Legal Basis: Non-essential cookies are processed based on user consent.
  • User Control: Users can manage cookie settings via browser configurations.
  • Third-Party Tracking: Analytical tools such as Google Analytics may be used to monitor website traffic.
  • Retention Policy: Cookies are stored for predefined periods and automatically deleted upon expiration.

For additional information, please refer to our Cookies Policy.

9. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell, lease, or trade personal data. However, data may be shared with:

  • Service Providers: External vendors assisting with business operations.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by applicable laws or regulations.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of mergers, acquisitions, or company restructuring.

All data-sharing activities are conducted with appropriate safeguards to ensure user data protection.
