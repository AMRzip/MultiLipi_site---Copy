Frequently Asked Questions

What types of files can MultiLipi translate?

MultiLipi supports a variety of file formats including PDFs, Word documents, Excel sheets, and more, allowing you to translate documents in the format you prefer.

How accurate is the AI-powered translation?

Our AI delivers highly accurate translations, and you can manually edit any part to ensure contextual accuracy and make refinements for specific needs.

Can I collaborate with my team on document translations?

Yes! MultiLipi allows multiple team members to work on the same project simultaneously, providing real-time collaboration for faster and more effective translations.

Is it possible to maintain consistency across large translation projects?

Absolutely. With our glossary and translation memory features, you can save key terms and reuse past translations to ensure consistency and save time across all your projects.

Do I have control over the translation process?

Yes, in addition to automatic translations, MultiLipi provides full editing capabilities so you can adjust and refine translations as needed.

How does MultiLipi handle data security?

We take data privacy seriously. All uploaded files are securely stored, and only authorized team members have access. We comply with industry-standard encryption protocols.

Can I track the progress of my translation projects?

Yes, MultiLipi provides detailed insights and progress tracking, allowing you to monitor project completion rates, team contributions, and other important statistics.

Is there a limit to the number of languages MultiLipi supports?

MultiLipi supports a wide range of languages, allowing you to translate documents into multiple languages for global audiences with ease. If you have specific language needs, feel free to reach out!
