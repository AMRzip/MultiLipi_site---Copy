How do I sign up for the MultiLipi Affiliate Program?
You can sign up instantly using the affiliate link on this page. Theres no waiting period you can start earning commissions right away. Note: MultiLipi reserves the right to reject any affiliate applications at its discretion without the obligation to provide a reason.
What commission rates do I earn?
Heres how your MultiLipi affiliate earnings work: 30% recurring commission on Starter plan referrals 15% commission on Agency and Unlimited plan signups 10% on all annual subscriptions you refer Example: Refer 100 Starter plan users, and you can earn over $4,000 in monthly recurring revenue as long as those users remain active.
When are payouts made?
Affiliate commissions are processed and paid the following month after they are earned. Minimum payout threshold is $150.
How long do MultiLipi referral cookies last?
Referral cookies remain active for 45 days after the first click. If the user clicks your link again, the 45-day window resets.
Who can I refer to MultiLipi?
You can refer any individual, agency, business, or organization that wants to make their website multilingual except yourself or your colleagues in the same company.
How will I get paid?
Affiliate payouts are made via PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer. Your rewards are calculated monthly and sent out after meeting the $150 threshold.
Can I use PPC or paid ads to promote MultiLipi?
No, paid advertising on any platform (PPC, search, or display) using MultiLipis brand name or domain is strictly prohibited. Violations will result in account termination and cancellation of commissions.
What if a customer downgrades or requests a refund?
Your commission will be adjusted accordingly. In cases of partial or full refunds, we reserve the right to reclaim associated commissions.
